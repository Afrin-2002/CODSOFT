Alappuzha

Alappuzha (or Alleppey) is a city on the Laccadive Sea in the southern Indian state of Kerala. It's best known for houseboat cruises along the rustic Kerala backwaters, a network of tranquil canals and lagoons.

District: Alappuzha district State: Kerala
Fee : Start from ₹800 per/hr
Rating : 4.5⭐

Munnar

Munnar is a town in the Western Ghats mountain range in India’s Kerala state. A hill station and former resort for the British Raj elite, it's surrounded by rolling hills dotted with tea plantations established in the late 19th century.

District: Idukki
State: Kerala
Fee : Start from ₹1500
Rating : 4.8⭐

Ooty

Ooty (short for Udhagamandalam) is a resort town in the Western Ghats mountains, in southern India's Tamil Nadu state. Founded as a British Raj summer resort, it retains a working steam railway line.

District: Nilgiris
State: TamilNadu
Rating : 4.7⭐




Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal is a hill town in the southern Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It’s set in an area of granite cliffs, forested valleys, lakes, waterfalls and grassy hills. At 2,000 meters above sea level, the town centers around man-made,bordered by evergreen forest.

District: Dindigul
State: TamilNadu
Rating : 4.6⭐

TajMahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the river Yamuna in Agra. It built in 1631 by the fifth Mughal emperor, Shah Jahan to house the tomb of his beloved wife, Mumtaz Mahal.

District: Agra
State: Uttar Pradesh
Fee : ₹50 for Indians & Foreigners pay 1100
Rating : 4.8⭐

Mysore Palace

Mysore Palace, also known as Amba Vilas Palace, is a historical palace and a royal residence. It used to be the official residence of the Wadiyar dynasty and the seat of the Kingdom of Mysore.

District: Mysuru
State: Karnataka
Fee : Start from ₹200
Rating : 4.5⭐

Kochi

kochi (also known as Cochin) is a city in southwest India's coastal Kerala state. It has been a port since 1341, when a flood carved out its harbor and opened it to Arab, Chinese and European merchants. Cantilevered Chinese fishing nets, typical of Kochi, have been in use for centuries.

District: Ernakulam
State: Kerala
Rating : 4.5⭐

Yercaud

Yercaud is a hill station town in the south Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It lies in the Shevaroy Hills, known for their orange groves and spice plantations. Yercaud Lake has boathouse and surrounded by gardens and woods.

District: salem
State: TamiNadu
Fee : Start from ₹30
Rating : 4.4⭐

Wayanad

Wayanad is a rural district in Kerala state, southwest India. In the east, the Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary is a lush, forested region with areas of high altitude, home to animals including Asiatic elephants, tigers, leopards and egrets.

District: Wayanad
State: Kerala
Rating : 4.5⭐
